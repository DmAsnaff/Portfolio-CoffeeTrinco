~Trendy Coffee~

At Coffee Trinco, we are passionate about delivering exceptional coffee products that inspire and delight.

Espresso

A concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely ground coffee beans. It forms the base for many other coffee drinks.

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a balanced and creamy coffee beverage.

Latte

Espresso combined with steamed milk, topped with a small layer of milk foam. It has a smooth and creamy texture.

Americano

A shot or two of espresso diluted with hot water, resembling the strength of traditional brewed coffee.

Macchiato

An espresso shot "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam. It provides a bolder and more concentrated flavor compared to a latte or cappuccino.

Mocha

A delightful blend of espresso, steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and topped with whipped cream. It offers a rich and indulgent flavor.
